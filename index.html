GET TO KNOW NAQ

Nsovo and Akani's Quality Smart Systems and Solutions is a company that was found by 2 young entrepreneurs, Nsovo Baloyi and Akani Keith Shivambu. NAQ Smart Systems and Solutions specializes in designing business logos(Branding), developing and designing websites, databases as well as developing and administrating financial softwares. One of the major hurdle in the business world that NAQ understands greatly is the problem of accumulating funds, especially for start up companies. That's why we at NAQ offer our services at an affordable price. We offer our services effectively and with great quality, we do this because keeping our clients happy and satisfied with our work is our number one priority before anything else.
NAQ Smart Systems and Solutions aims to help small or start up companies with the designing of Logos and Business cards, developing and designing of websites(also applicable to advanced companies). We also help those advanced Companies with the developing and designing of databases and financial softwares as well as administrating it. We have a firm belief that the services that we offer are Pivotal in all business levels and can be used greatly in the day-to-day running of your business.
NAQ Smart Systems and Solutions believes to be different and better because not only do we target advanced companies, we also target small or start up companies and with affordable prices. We also help and encourage these start up business in whatever way we could as we have a firm belief that big companies that you see everyday in the business world started off as 'Small or Start up companies'.
